# Branding Specification

## Design Direction

Planning-tool focused. Blue-dominant for trust and structure; orange reserved exclusively for CTAs. Draws from both Monarch Money (orange) and MaxiFi (blue) brand identities while establishing a distinct combined identity.

---

## Color Palette

All combinations meet WCAG AA accessibility standards (4.5:1 minimum contrast for normal text, 3:1 for large text and UI components).

| Role | Hex | Contrast | WCAG |
|---|---|---|---|
| Navy — nav, headers, brand | `#0F2D5E` | 12.6:1 vs white | AAA |
| Blue — secondary actions, links | `#2D6BB5` | 5.1:1 w/ white text | AA |
| Orange — CTAs only | `#C4401F` | 5.1:1 w/ white text | AA |
| Success / positive values | `#157A55` | 5.3:1 w/ white text | AA |
| Warning / over-budget | `#F59E0B` | 7.7:1 w/ dark text | AA |
| Background | `#F7F8FA` | — | — |
| Card surface | `#FFFFFF` | — | — |
| Primary text | `#1A1F2E` | 14:1 vs white | AAA |
| Secondary text | `#5B6370` | 5.2:1 vs white | AA |
| Borders / dividers | `#E2E8F0` | — | — |

### Usage rules

- **Orange (`#C4401F`)** is for primary CTA buttons only — not decorative use, not headings, not links.
- **Amber (`#F59E0B`)** must always use dark text (`#1A1F2E`). Never pair with white text.
- **Navy (`#0F2D5E`)** is the dominant brand color: top nav, sidebar, page headers.
- **Blue (`#2D6BB5`)** is for secondary interactive elements: links, secondary buttons, active states.

---

## Typography

**Font family:** Inter (Google Fonts)

Inter is the standard for data-dense fintech dashboards — highly legible at small sizes, excellent number rendering, and free.

| Role | Weight | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Page headings | 700 (Bold) | Navy `#0F2D5E` |
| Section headings | 600 (SemiBold) | Navy `#0F2D5E` |
| Body text | 400 (Regular) | Primary text `#1A1F2E` |
| Labels / captions | 400 (Regular) | Secondary text `#5B6370` |
| CTA buttons | 600 (SemiBold) | White on orange |
| Data / numbers | 500 (Medium) | Tabular nums feature enabled |

## Brand Sources

- **Monarch Money** — primary orange reference; original brand orange `#E2522F` (deepened to `#C4401F` for AA compliance)
- **MaxiFi** — blue-dominant planning tool aesthetic; professional, data-focused
